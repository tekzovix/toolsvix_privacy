Introduction

Welcome to Toolsvix, developed and operated by Tekzovix, based in Pakistan. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application available on Android (and iOS when published).

By downloading and using Toolsvix, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

💡 Toolsvix is designed with a privacy-first approach. Most PDF and utility tools process your files on your device. Text is only sent over the internet when you use online features such as Translate, AI Summarizer, or Grammar & Rewriter.

Current app version (v1.0) includes: PDF tools, AI & Voice tools (summarize, grammar, translate, OCR), and File & Text tools (QR, converters, etc.). Image and Media tool sections are not included in this release.

How We Use Information

The limited information we interact with is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Tool functionality — processing PDFs, text, and files on your device to deliver outputs
  • AI-powered features — sending text to Google Gemini API for summarization, grammar fixing, and rewriting
  • Online translation — sending text to MyMemory (mymemory.translated.net) for Translate and PDF Translate features
  • On-device vision — using Google ML Kit for OCR (image-to-text) and QR code scanning on your device
⚠️ When you use AI tools (Summarizer, Grammar & Rewriter), your text is sent to Google's Gemini API. When you use Translate or PDF Translate, your text is sent to MyMemory. We do not store this text on our servers, but each provider's privacy policy applies. See: policies.google.com/privacy and mymemory.translated.net/doc/terms.php
✅ Advertising: The current version (v1.0) does not display ads. If ads are enabled in a future update via Google AdMob, this policy will be updated and the Play Store Data safety section will be revised accordingly.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since Toolsvix does not create user accounts or store personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to retain on our infrastructure. Specifically:

  • Files you process are not uploaded to Tekzovix servers — they stay on your device unless a tool sends text to an online API
  • Text sent to Gemini or MyMemory is processed by those services — we do not store copies
  • Output files are saved to your device's storage — you control them entirely

To delete all local app data: go to your phone's Settings → Apps → Toolsvix → Clear Data. Uninstalling the app removes all local data completely.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • API keys are injected at build time — not stored in public source code
  • No user credentials are ever stored (no accounts)
  • On-device tools (PDF, OCR, QR) do not pass through Tekzovix servers

While we implement strong protections, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use the app responsibly and avoid submitting highly sensitive information through internet-connected AI or translation tools.
