How We Use Information

The limited information we interact with is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Tool functionality — processing your PDFs, images, audio, and text to deliver the tool's output
  • AI-powered features — sending text to the Gemini API (Google) for summarization, grammar fixing, translation, and rewriting
  • Advertising — displaying ads via Google AdMob to keep the app free for all users
  • App improvement — anonymous crash reports to identify and fix bugs
⚠️ When you use AI tools (Summarizer, Grammar Fixer, Translator, Rewriter), your input text is sent to Google's Gemini API. We do not store this text, but Google's own privacy policy applies to that transmission. See: policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention & Deletion

Since Toolsvix does not create user accounts or store personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to retain. Specifically:

  • Files you process are never uploaded to our servers — they stay on your device
  • Text submitted to AI tools is processed and immediately discarded — not stored by Tekzovix
  • Output files are saved to your device's storage — you control them entirely
  • Anonymous crash logs are retained for 90 days then automatically deleted

To delete all app data: go to your phone's Settings → Apps → Toolsvix → Clear Data. Uninstalling the app removes all local data completely.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • API keys are compiled into the app binary — never exposed in source code
  • No user credentials are ever stored (no accounts)
  • Files processed on-device never touch our infrastructure

While we implement strong protections, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use the app responsibly and not process highly sensitive documents through internet-connected AI tools.
